A very nice 3 egg ham and cheese (or whatever) omlette

Description

This is a wonderfully fluffy three-egg omlette that is easy, fast, and very simple to make. I used a little ham and cheese because I'm a traditionalist, but we can add whatever we like. Thats the fun in omlettes!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 TBsp milk or water
  • 2-3 slices or 1/4 cup cheese
  • about 1/2 cup sliced deli ham

Instructions

  1. Begin by prepping your pan by placing it on a medium heat. A little butter or oil will help keep your omlette from sticking, and make your life easier down the road.
  2. Prepare a small bowl, and crack your eggs into it. Add the milk to the eggs and whisk/beat them until smooth
  3. Once you're satisfied that the pan has reached a good temperature, pour the eggs on!
  4. Give it a few moments to set, and then add the cheese. I like to add the cheese at this point as a sort of reinforcement for the eggs
  5. Once the cheese begins to melt, you're ready to add the ham
  6. let it cook for about a minute
  7. take a spatula, and fold the omlette over itself. Depending on the size of the pan, you may want to do a tri-fold by folding either end of the meal toward the middle.
  8. Let it sit for about another half a minute, then give it a nice flip. It will be easier to tilt the pan, this isn't a four egg omlette after all! (gosh could you imagine?)
  9. Let it cook for another minute or so. Use the browning you see to gauge how you're doing, it's your omlette after all!
  10. Plate, add a little salt and pepper (or more if you're feeling bold) and serve!
  11. Enjoy!
